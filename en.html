How to Find Our Accommodation

By Public Transportation

  • Subway: 15-minute walk from Exit #4 of Suwon Station
  • Bus: Take bus #11, #13, or #36 from Suwon Station and get off at Suwoncheon Bus Stop, then 3-minute walk
  • Taxi: About 5-7 minutes from Suwon Station (approx. 2.5km)

By Car

  • Enter "Chaeum Accommodation" or address "15-10, Suwoncheon-ro 407beon-gil, Jangan-gu, Suwon-si, Gyeonggi-do" in your navigation
  • Parking available in front of the accommodation (5,000 won per night)
