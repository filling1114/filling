Directions to the Accommodation

🚌 By Public Transportation

  • Bus: Take bus #35 from Suwon Station (Yeokjeon Market) stop, get off at Gyoyukcheongsageori, then walk 3 minutes (approx. 29 min).
  • Taxi: Approximately 10-20 minutes from Suwon Station (about 4km).

🚗 By Car

  • Enter the address "36, Songwon-ro 86beon-gil, Jangan-gu, Suwon-si, Gyeonggi-do" into your navigation system.
  • Parking: Jangan-gu Office Parking available (₩7,000 per day).
